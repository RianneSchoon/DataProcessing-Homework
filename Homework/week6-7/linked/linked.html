These are two interactively linked visualizations!
Selecting data in one part has an effect on the other part.
Hovering over the data also works in both views!

The world map shows the homicide rates per country.
The scatter plot then shows the Life Expectancy of that country,
as well as the Happy Life Years, and the Gross Domestic Product
Data are plotted by continent, to facilitate country comparison.

Data source homicides: The World Bank
Data source happiness: Happy Planet Index

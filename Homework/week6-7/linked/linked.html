These are two interactively linked visualizations!

The world map shows the number of intentional homicides per 100.000 people.
Hover over a country on the map to see the country name and murder information pop up.
Feel free to zoom in and out, or to drag the map to your place of interest!

The scatter plot below the map shows the life expectancy of some countries,
as well as the happy life years, and the gross domestic product (GDP).
As you can see, people live longer, happier lives when their countries GDP is higher (bigger dots).
Hover over the dots to see the country name and its information pop up.
The scattered dots are colored by continent, to facilitate country comparison.
Click on the legend to select data per continent, click two times to visualize all data again!

Data source homicides: The World Bank
Data source happiness: Happy Planet Index

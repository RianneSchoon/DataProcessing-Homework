Welcome to this visualization of happiness in life across the world!

This is an interactive scatterplot!

Click on the legend to select the region of which you want to see the happiness.
The selected data now pops out! Click the legend again to unselect or select other data.
Hover over the dots to see that country and its Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in dollars.
Hovering is also possible while a subset of the data is selected.
Note that GDP is translated to the size of the dots.

Data source: happyplanetindex.org
